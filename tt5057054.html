Did You Know?

Trivia

John Krasinski becomes the fifth actor to play Jack Ryan and, like all the other replacements in between, has appeared in at least one film with the original, Alec Baldwin. Krasinski and Baldwin appeared in Deu a Louca nos Astros (2000), Simplesmente Complicado (2009), and Sob o Mesmo Céu (2015). See more »
