Wes Anderson’s ‘Isle Of Dogs’ To Open 2018 Berlin Film Festival

  • 15 minutes ago
  • by Kevin Jagernauth
  • The Playlist
Sometimes, it’s not worth messing with the recipe for success. Nearly four years ago, Wes Anderson debuted “The Grand Budapest Hotel” at the Berlin International Film Festival where it earned rave reviews, and went on to become the biggest global box office success by a healthy margin with $174.8 million (the next closest is “The Royal Tenenbaums” with $71.4 million). So, for his next film, the director and Fox Searchlight are hoping to repeat history.
