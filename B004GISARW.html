Product description

IMDb, the world’s most popular and authoritative source for movie, TV, and celebrity content.

Sign in to:
• Add movies and TV shows to your Watchlist
• Rate what you’ve seen
• Receive notifications for showtimes, breaking news, and more

Explore and search:
• Our catalog of 3+ million movie and TV titles
• Over 7+ million credited celebrities, actors, actresses, directors, and crew members
• Current movie trailers and entertainment news
• Movie & TV showtimes and user & critic reviews
• Quotes, trivia, and goofs
• Lists of Best Picture winners, top-rated and most popular movies and TV, celebrity birthdays, and more

IMDb is available worldwide in English (US/UK), Spanish, German, French, Portuguese, Italian, Japanese, Korean, and Chinese.
