Did You Know?

Trivia

This is the second Blue Sky Studios film based on a book and animation to have an original theme music being remade by its film composer. The first is The Peanuts Movie's (2015) "Linus and Lucy", "Skating", "Christmas Time Is Here", and "Christmas is Coming" being remade by Christophe Beck. The second is Ferdinand's (2017) "Flower Theme" being remade by John Powell. See more »
