Mina Tander

Actress | Soundtrack
View Resume | Official Photos »
Mina Tander was born on December 4, 1979 in Cologne, North Rhine-Westphalia, West Germany. She is an actress, known for Berlin Station (2016), Du hast es versprochen (2012) and Maria, ihm schmeckt's nicht! (2009). She is married to Elmar Fischer. They have one child. See full bio »
