Elisha Cuthbert

Actress | Director | Producer
Elisha Ann Cuthbert (born November 30, 1982) is a Canadian actress and model. She became internationally known for playing Kim Bauer in the series 24 Horas (2001); Danielle in the teen comedy film Show de Vizinha (2004) and Carly Jones in the 2005 in of A Casa de Cera (2005).
