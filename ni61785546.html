Oscars to Start Half Hour Earlier, May Finally End Before Dawn (Video)

  • 33 minutes ago
  • by Tony Maglio
  • The Wrap
East-coasters may actually be able to stay awake for Best Picture this year: the 90th Oscars will begin a half-hour earlier, starting at 8 p.m. out by the Atlantic Ocean instead of 8:30. The 2018 Academy Awards will be held on Sunday, March 4 at the Dolby Theatre in Hollywood. They’ll again be televised on ABC. As previously announced, late-night talk show favorite Jimmy Kimmel will return to host, and Michael De Luca and Jennifer Todd will return to produce.
